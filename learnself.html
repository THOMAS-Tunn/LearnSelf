Help 💡
How LearnSelf works.
Adding an assignment
Click the + button on the Dashboard. Fill in the Name, Due Date, and Difficulty — these are required. Class, Description, and Assign Date are optional.
Priority system
Red = due today. Yellow = due in 2–3 days. Green = 1 week or more. The priority number is calculated from due date and difficulty.
Bulk actions
Check the top checkbox to select all rows. Then use Mark Finished or Delete that appear above the table.
Viewing details
Click any row in the Dashboard to see the full assignment details in a popup.
